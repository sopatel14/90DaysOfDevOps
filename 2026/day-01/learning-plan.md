
## 90-Day DevOps Learning Plan

### Current Level

Working Professional with 2+ years of experience in IT Support.
Currently transitioning into DevOps & Cloud Engineering to build stronger technical skills and move toward better remote job opportunities.

---

## My Understanding of DevOps & Cloud Engineering

DevOps is a mindset and process that brings Development and Operations teams together to deliver applications faster, more reliably, and with minimal downtime. A DevOps Engineer ensures system stability, automation, monitoring, and smooth deployments in production environments.

Cloud Engineering helps organizations build scalable, highly available, and cost-effective infrastructure using cloud platforms like AWS.

---

## Why I Am Learning DevOps & Cloud

I am starting DevOps and Cloud learning to upgrade my career from IT Support into a high-growth technical role.
My goal is to upskill myself, work on real-world projects, and become capable of getting high-paying remote opportunities in the DevOps field.

---

## My 90-Day Goals

1. Improve my fundamentals in Linux, Networking, Git, and Cloud.
2. Stay consistent and complete the 90 Days DevOps Challenge without skipping.
3. Deploy production-grade applications and projects that can attract hiring companies and strengthen my resume.

## Core Skills I Want to Build
